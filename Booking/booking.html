Halloween Event

            

Clover Hollow Farm near Athenry transforms into a spooky wonderland each Halloween, hosting its annual Clover Hollow Halloween Festival from October 25th to 31st. Families and thrill-seekers alike flock to the farm for its eerie Pumpkin Hollow Trail, where hundreds of carved pumpkins light the way through a misty forest path. The Haunted Hayride takes guests on a chilling journey through the Forgotten Fields, where animatronic creatures and ghostly figures lurk in the shadows. Children can get creative in the Witch’s Kitchen Workshop, decorating cupcakes and brewing edible potions, while the Barnyard Boogie offers a glow-in-the-dark costume dance party with live music and fog machines. Adventurers can test their nerves in the Scarecrow Maze, a twisty corn labyrinth patrolled by mischievous scarecrows. Tickets range from €15 for general admission to €25 for the VIP Spook Pass, which includes priority hayride access, a free pumpkin, and a hot cider voucher. With food trucks, a Monster Market, and even costumed goats in the petting zoo, Clover Hollow Farm promises a hauntingly wholesome experience for all ages.
