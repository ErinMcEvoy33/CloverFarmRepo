Countryside Café

Welcome to Countryside Café

Come join us in our Countryside Café — a cosy retreat nestled right in the heart of our farm.

Here, you can enjoy homemade meals crafted with fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced directly from our own gardens and local partners. Whether you’re savoring a warm slice of farmhouse pie or relaxing with a freshly brewed cup of coffee after meeting our friendly animals, our café offers the perfect blend of wholesome food, rural charm, and down-to-earth hospitality.
